## Decisions Made
- YouTube vaults use a simpler search-only toolbar since sort is handled by clickable column headers in YouTubeVideoList, avoiding the VaultSearchFilter's fixed sort field dropdown
- Column visibility uses responsive breakpoints (md/lg/xl) so the row degrades gracefully on smaller screens
- Outlier rank shows a dashed-border dash with tooltip rather than empty space to look intentionally disabled
- Video click navigates to `/call/{legacy_recording_id}` as temporary solution until Plan 04 adds modal
