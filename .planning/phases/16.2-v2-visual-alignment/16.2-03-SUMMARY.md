- **Dialog/modal titles stay text-foreground:** AddSourceDialog, YouTube dialog titles are modal headings, not page titles. They correctly keep text-foreground.
- **Sub-headings untouched:** h2/h3 within content (Summary, Transcript, Your Organizations, Upload Files) remain text-muted-foreground or text-foreground — they are content structure, not wayfinding.
