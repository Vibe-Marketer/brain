title: "Inviting Members"
description: "Invite teammates to your organization and give them access to the workspaces they need."

Adding someone to CallVault is a two-step process: first invite them to the organization, then add them to the specific workspaces they should have access to.

## Step 1: Invite to the organization

1. Go to **Settings → Organization → Members**
2. Click **Invite member**
3. Enter their email address
4. Choose their organization role: `organization_admin` or `member` (see [Organizations](/core-concepts/organizations) for what each role can do)
5. Click **Send invite**

The person will receive an email with a link to accept the invitation and set up their account. If they already have a CallVault account, accepting the invite will add them to your organization.

<Note>
  Inviting someone to the organization doesn't automatically give them access to any workspaces — that's a separate step.
</Note>

## Step 2: Add to workspaces

Once the person has accepted their org invite (or if you want to pre-configure access before they accept):

1. Open the workspace you want to add them to
2. Click **⋯ → Workspace Settings → Members**
3. Click **Add member**
4. Search for the person by name or email
5. Choose their workspace role: **Viewer**, **Editor**, or **Admin**
6. Click **Add**

Repeat for each workspace they need access to.

<Tip>
  Use [Teams](/collaboration/teams) to manage workspace access for groups of people at once — instead of adding and removing individuals one by one.
</Tip>

## Pending invitations

If someone hasn't accepted their invite yet, their status will show as **Pending** in the member list. You can:

- **Resend** the invitation email if they didn't receive it
- **Cancel** the invitation if you sent it to the wrong address

## Removing a member

To remove someone from your organization:

1. Go to **Settings → Organization → Members**
2. Find the member and click **Remove**

This removes them from the organization and from all workspaces. It does not delete any calls or content they created.

To remove someone from a single workspace without removing them from the org:

1. Open the workspace
2. Go to **⋯ → Workspace Settings → Members**
3. Click the **⋯** next to their name and select **Remove from workspace**

## Changing a member's role

You can update someone's org-level role or workspace-level role at any time:

- **Org role**: **Settings → Organization → Members → [member] → Change role**
- **Workspace role**: **Workspace Settings → Members → [member] → Change role**

Changes take effect immediately.
