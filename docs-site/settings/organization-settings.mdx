Organization settings control everything at the org level — who's a member, what roles they have, what the org is called, and what plan you're on.

Access organization settings at **Settings → Organization**. You need to be an `organization_owner` or `organization_admin` to make changes here.

## General

The **General** section lets you:

- **Rename the organization** — change the display name shown in the nav and on invoices
- **Organization URL** — set a custom slug for your org (used in shared URLs)
- **Organization logo** — upload a logo that appears in shared call views and exports

## Members

The **Members** tab shows everyone who belongs to your organization and their org-level roles.

### Inviting members

Click **Invite member**, enter an email address, and choose a role:

- `organization_owner` — full control including billing and org deletion
- `organization_admin` — manage members and settings, but not billing
- `member` — can be added to workspaces but has no org-level management access

See [Inviting Members](/collaboration/inviting-members) for the full workflow.

### Changing roles

Click the role badge next to any member to change their org-level role. Changes take effect immediately.

### Removing members

Click **⋯ → Remove from organization** next to any member. This removes them from the org and all workspaces. It does not delete their call uploads.

## Teams

The **Teams** tab is where you create and manage teams — named groups of members that can be added to workspaces together. See [Teams](/collaboration/teams).

## Integrations

The **Integrations** tab shows all connected call sources (Fathom, Zoom) and their status. Each org member manages their own integration connections — this view gives admins visibility into what's connected.

## Billing

The **Billing** tab is visible to `organization_owner` only. It shows:

- Your current plan (Free, Starter, Business, Enterprise)
- Seat count and usage
- Next billing date and amount
- A link to update your payment method or change your plan
- Downloadable invoices for past billing periods

To upgrade, click **Upgrade plan** and follow the prompts. Changes to your plan take effect immediately.

## Danger zone

The **Danger zone** at the bottom of General settings contains:

- **Delete organization** — permanently deletes the org, all workspaces, all calls, and all member associations

<Warning>
  Deleting an organization is irreversible. All calls, transcripts, recordings, AI summaries, and workspace data are permanently deleted. There is no recovery option. Download any important exports before proceeding.
</Warning>
